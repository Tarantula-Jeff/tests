About Us

Philip Acquah, also known as Cyril Lisk, is a talented and professional photographer who began his photography career at a young age. Over the years, he has honed his skills and gained extensive experience by working with notable clients, including celebrities like Serwaa Amihere and Ahuofe Patri, as well as international clients who book his services locally. Philip’s expertise extends to capturing professional moments for renowned companies such as Guinness, among others. Passionate about his craft, Philip strives to create beautifully garnished pictures that preserve cherished memories for his clients. He is deeply committed to delivering excellence, making every moment he captures truly special. His dedication and sacrifices reflect his deep love and respect for every client. Beyond photography, Philip has a keen interest in fashion and the beauty of natural landscapes. Guided by his belief that “With God, all things are possible,” he continues to bring his creative vision to life with passion and purpose.
